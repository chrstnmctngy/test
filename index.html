  • Humba

    Steps:

    1. Heat a wok or pan. Sear pork belly until it turns brown and enough oil gets extracted from the fat. Remove the pork from the wok. Set aside.
    2. Heat around 3 tablespoons of oil. Saute garlic and onion until the oinion softens.
    3. Add the pork back into the wok and pour-in soy sauce. Saute for 1 minute
    4. Add vinegar. Continue sauteing until liquid dries up.
    5. Add brown sugar. Stir. Cook for 1 minute
    6. Transfer to a serving plate. Serve with warm rice.
